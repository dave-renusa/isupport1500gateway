A Better Neighbor for Fredericksburg.

The 1500 Gateway Data Center Campus is the lower-impact option for our city, delivering transformative benefits without the burdens of traditional development.

Direct Funding for Our Schools

This project provides a 19% increase in local school funding, generating $6.3 million in new yearly revenue for the district.

  • $1,762 Additional Investment: Every student receives this extra funding, every single year.
  • $22,909 Per Student: Total additional investment over a single student's K-12 career.
  • Zero School Overcrowding: Unlike residential development, the data center generates 0 new students.
  • Zero Strain: Provides massive funding while requiring no new classrooms or bus routes.

Compare this to typical mixed-use development, which would add roughly 228 students to our system while generating only a fraction of the tax revenue.
