Our Story

Responsible Growth, Enduring Value

Since founding Penzance in 1996, good corporate citizenship has been central to who we are and how we work — nearly three decades guided by a simple but powerful commitment: leaving things better than we found them.

Every day, our team strives to enhance sustainability, steward the environment, and strengthen the communities where we live and work. We are proud of the workplace culture we've built — one rooted in camaraderie, collaboration, and excellence — and equally proud of the care we bring to our tenants, residents, neighbors, and partners.

Since our inception, Penzance has invested over $1.4 billion in multifamily, office, data center, and industrial properties across the Commonwealth of Virginia, with another $3.7 billion in the pipeline — building a long track record of developing and operating spaces where people and businesses can thrive.

From energy efficiency initiatives and LEED certifications to programs designed to enhance the everyday experience of those who live and work in our buildings, our commitment to responsible growth shows up in everything we do.

We are passionate about real estate — acquiring it, developing it, building it, and managing it — and even more passionate about the positive impact it can have on the communities we serve.

Penzance is the developer behind the 1500 Gateway Data Campus in Fredericksburg, Virginia — a project designed to be a transformative, low-impact neighbor for the community.
