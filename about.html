Our Story

Responsible Growth, Enduring Value

Penzance is committed to creating enduring value for our investors, partners, tenants, and the communities we serve. This commitment guides our decisions and long-term approach to responsible growth.

Since our founding in 1996, Penzance has invested more than $4.5 billion in gross asset value across a diversified real estate portfolio. We have long-standing relationships with leading institutional investors, separate accounts, and joint venture partners.

In addition, Penzance manages approximately $1.0 billion in discretionary capital through the flagship Penzance Real Estate Funds, reflecting our disciplined investment philosophy, integrated operations, and laser focus on risk mitigation.

With a strong foundation in the Mid-Atlantic region, we source and acquire value-add opportunities across multifamily, industrial, data center, and select commercial assets. Our development and operating platforms allow us to evaluate opportunities holistically, manage risk effectively, and execute business plans with precision — supporting resilient performance across market cycles.

A key differentiator for Penzance is sourcing more than half of our investments through off-market transactions, driven by deep and proprietary industry relationships and market insight. Together, these strengths allow us to identify and exploit market inefficiencies to pursue investments that align with our return objectives and long-term responsibility to stakeholders.

*Performance numbers are since inception. Past performance is not indicative of future results. All results are gross and do not reflect the customary fees and expenses investors pay when investing in pooled investment vehicles, such as management and performance fees. As of September 30, 2025.

Penzance is the developer behind the 1500 Gateway Data Campus in Fredericksburg, Virginia — a project designed to be a transformative, low-impact neighbor for the community.
